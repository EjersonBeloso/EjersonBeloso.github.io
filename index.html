Libjo Island

Dinagat Islands

South

Kisses Islets

If you have been to El Nido or want to visit the counterpart in the east, you should take a look at Libjo, Dinagat Islands. Here you can find the hidden gems of Kisses Islets, which are at least as beautiful, but at least twice as difficult to reach. Besides the jaw-dropping limestone, the crystal-clear turquoise waters protected by the striking little islets are calm and beguiling. Dinagat in itself is also well-known for its beaches, resorts, and caves.

Carbon Market

One of the oldest urban markets in the country, it surprises us again and again that many travelers avoid the Carbon market or are even afraid to visit it. This is a local affair and a very nice opportunity to experience Philippine everyday life up close and personal. Also, other famous sights like Magellan's Cross or Basilica Sto. Nino is only a few minutes walk away.
